# Privacy Policy for HABA

**Last Updated:** 5 March 2026

HABA ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the HABA attendance application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Location Data (Foreground Location)
We collect and process your precise location data (GPS coordinates) **only when you actively use the application** to perform check-in or check-out for attendance purposes.
- **Why we need it:** To verify that you are within the designated office perimeter before confirming your attendance.
- **How it works:** The application will request permission to access your location ("While using the app"). If granted, location data is only accessed momentarily when you tap the attendance button.
- **Data Retention:** We do not track your location continuously in the background. Your location coordinates at the time of check-in/check-out are sent securely to our servers to validate the attendance record.

### b. Biometric Data
We use the biometric features of your device (Fingerprint, Face ID, etc.) to securely authenticate your access to the application.
- **Why we need it:** To provide a secure and convenient login method.
- **How it works:** Biometric data is processed locally on your device using the secure hardware. We do not collect, transmit, or store your biometric data on our servers.

### c. Device Information
We may collect basic device information, such as the device ID or token, to manage sessions and send important push notifications (e.g., attendance reminders, company announcements).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:
- Verifying your location during the attendance process.
- Authenticating your identity to access the application.
- Providing core app functionalities (e.g., displaying prayer times based on your current location).
- Sending important notifications relevant to your work.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your attendance records and location data are securely transmitted to our internal HR servers and are accessible only by authorized personnel for management purposes.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Depending on the features enabled, the app may interact with third-party APIs (such as equran.id for prayer times or Nominatim for reverse geocoding). In these cases, your location data may be sent to these services solely to retrieve location-specific public data (like prayer schedules). These services do not store your data for tracking purposes.

## 5. Security

We implement adequate security measures to protect your information, including encrypting your local session tokens using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

## 6. Access and Changes

You can revoke location and biometric permissions at any time through your device’s settings. However, please note that doing so may prevent you from using the core features of the app, such as checking in or logging in securely.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact your HR or IT department.
