====== BETA APP DESCRIPTION (English) ======

HABA - Mobile Presence is a corporate mobile attendance application designed for Bank Aceh employees. This app enables staff to perform GPS-based check-in and check-out directly from their mobile devices.

Key Features:
- GPS-based attendance with geofencing validation
- Real-time attendance history and reporting
- Prayer time reminders (Adzan notification)
- Temporary relocation support for auditors
- App version update notifications
- Secure login with device binding (JWT authentication)

What to Test:
- Check-in and check-out flow using GPS location
- Attendance history viewing
- Prayer time schedule accuracy
- Profile management
- Location permission consent flow
- App update notification behavior

Requirements:
- Active internet connection
- GPS/Location services enabled
- Valid employee credentials provided by administrator

Notes:
This is an internal corporate app for Bank Aceh staff. Test accounts will be provided by the administrator. Please ensure location services are enabled for full functionality testing.


====== WHAT TO TEST (Indonesian) ======

Silakan uji fitur-fitur berikut pada build ini:

1. Absensi (Check-in & Check-out)
   - Pastikan GPS aktif, lalu lakukan absensi masuk dan pulang
   - Periksa apakah validasi lokasi (geofencing) berjalan dengan benar

2. Riwayat Absensi
   - Buka halaman riwayat, pastikan data kehadiran tampil dengan akurat

3. Jadwal Sholat & Pengingat
   - Periksa apakah jadwal waktu sholat sesuai dengan lokasi Anda

4. Izin Lokasi (Location Permission)
   - Saat pertama kali membuka aplikasi, pastikan muncul dialog permintaan izin lokasi
   - Uji jika izin ditolak, aplikasi tetap berjalan normal

5. Update Aplikasi
   - Pastikan notifikasi update muncul dengan benar saat versi baru tersedia

6. Login & Keamanan
   - Login menggunakan akun yang telah disediakan oleh administrator
   - Pastikan binding perangkat berfungsi dengan baik

Catatan: Akun pengujian disediakan oleh administrator. Pastikan koneksi internet dan GPS dalam keadaan aktif.
